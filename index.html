Biography

Senior ICT manager with over 24 years of experience in Telecommunications, IT and Cybersecurity. Capable of managing budgets, recruiting new personnel, meeting business targets and moving towards new business directions with AI. I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in electrical engineering from King Saud University, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, in 2001 and 2006, respectively. In 2020, I received the Ph.D. degree in electrical and computer engineering from Concordia University, Montreal, QC, Canada. My research interests include 5G Wireless Networks, Millimeter-wave, Microwave components, Antennas, Video/Image processing, Dynamic Encryption, Data Science, Applied AI, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.
